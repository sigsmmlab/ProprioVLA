\section{Related Work}

\paragraph{Vision-language-action models.}
Discuss prior work on language-conditioned robotic policies and generalist VLA
models.

\paragraph{Proprioception for robot learning.}
Discuss how robot state, proprioceptive feedback, and embodiment information
are used in visuomotor control and robot foundation models.

\paragraph{Multimodal policy learning.}
Position \textsc{ProprioVLA} relative to methods that combine visual, language,
state, and action modalities.
